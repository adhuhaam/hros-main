Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the RCC Employee App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, employee ID, contact information, photo, and work-related information.
  • Authentication Info: Username and password for secure access.
  • App Usage Data: Log data, device info, and interaction patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate employees securely.
  • To display payroll, attendance, leave, and profile data.
  • To improve app features and performance.
  • To send important HR notifications.

3. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold. It may be shared only with authorized RCC HR personnel for HR operations and legal compliance.

4. Data Security

We implement strict measures to protect your data using secure API protocols, encrypted connections, and access control mechanisms.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and view your personal data.
  • Request corrections to incorrect information.
  • Request data deletion (subject to HR policy and legal obligations).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.

7. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact RCC HR Department via the in-app chat or email at hr@rccmaldives.com.
